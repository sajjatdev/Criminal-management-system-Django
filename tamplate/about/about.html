Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) is one of the most important police units of Bangladesh Police. DMP is entrusted to provide excellent policing service to the city dwellers with active community participation an engagement. It mainly works in the areas of low enforcement, crime prevention, investigation, public order management, internal security, traffic management and social integration. Every day a lot of crimes are occurs in our country. DMP is committed to make Dhaka city safer. The goal of the Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) is to ensure public safety and maintain law and order in Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh. The DMP aims to provide a safe and secure environment for the citizens and visitors of Dhaka by preventing and controlling crime, enforcing traffic regulations, and responding to emergencies promptly. To achieve its goal, the DMP has identified the following objectives: 1. Preventing and controlling crime: The DMP aims to prevent crime by implementing effective crime prevention measures such as increasing police visibility in public places, conducting regular patrols, and providing safety advice to the public. The DMP also seeks to control crime by apprehending criminals, conducting investigations, and prosecuting offenders. 2. Ensuring public safety: The DMP is committed to ensuring the safety of the public by responding to emergencies such as accidents, natural disasters, and terrorist threats promptly. The DMP also provides safety advice and education to the public to prevent accidents and other safety hazards. 3. Enforcing traffic regulations: The DMP aims to ensure the safety of road users by enforcing traffic regulations such as speed limits, seatbelt use, and driving under the influence of drugs or alcohol. The DMP also conducts awareness campaigns to educate the public about safe driving practices and the importance of following traffic rules. 4. Building community trust: The DMP recognizes the importance of building trust and cooperation between the police and the community. The DMP seeks to engage with the community by establishing community policing programs, conducting outreach activities, and listening to community concerns and feedback. By achieving these objectives, the DMP aims to create a safe and secure environment for the citizens and visitors of Dhaka, promoting social harmony and economic growth in the city.
